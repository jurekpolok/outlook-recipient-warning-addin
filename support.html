Thank you for using the Recipient Privacy Warning Add-in!

How to Use

  1. Compose a new email in Outlook
  2. Add recipients to the To, CC, or BCC fields
  3. Click the "Check Recipients" button in the ribbon
  4. Review the recipient count and any warnings displayed
  5. If warned, consider moving external recipients to BCC

Frequently Asked Questions

Why should I use BCC for external recipients?

When you add recipients to To or CC, all recipients can see each other's email addresses. Using BCC (Blind Carbon Copy) hides recipients from each other, protecting their privacy and complying with data protection regulations like GDPR.

What triggers the warning?

The add-in warns you when you have more than 5 recipients in the To and CC fields combined. This threshold helps identify mass emails that might benefit from using BCC.

What counts as an "external" recipient?

External recipients are email addresses that don't belong to your organization's configured domains. Internal recipients are those with email addresses from your company's domain.
