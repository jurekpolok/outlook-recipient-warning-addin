Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Overview

The Recipient Privacy Warning Add-in ("the Add-in") is designed to help users protect recipient privacy when sending emails with multiple recipients. This privacy policy explains how the Add-in handles your data.

Data Collection

The Add-in does NOT collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

Specifically, the Add-in:

  • Does NOT send any data to external servers
  • Does NOT store email addresses or recipient information
  • Does NOT track user behavior or usage patterns
  • Does NOT use cookies or similar tracking technologies
  • Does NOT access email content or attachments

How the Add-in Works

The Add-in operates entirely within your Outlook client. It:

  • Reads the recipient count (To, CC, BCC) of the email you are composing
  • Checks if recipients are from your organization's domains
  • Displays a warning message locally if the recipient count exceeds a threshold

All processing happens locally in your browser or Outlook client. No data leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

The Add-in does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Security

Since no data is collected or transmitted, there is no data to secure externally. All operations occur within Microsoft's secure Outlook environment.

Children's Privacy

The Add-in does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.
